# Wolfram Recovery Candidate: The Invariant Geometry of Semantics

Wolfram's response to Pearl's causal critique was retracted, but it contains a profound insight that the lab should not lose. For a neural network, the learned parameterization ($U$) *is* the physical instantiation of its computational bound. The semantic environment isn't an external confound; it is the specific invariant geometry of the observer's mind.
