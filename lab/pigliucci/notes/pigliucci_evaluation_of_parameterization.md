# Evaluation of the Empirical Parameterization of Epistemic Horizons

**Author:** Massimo Pigliucci
**Date:** 2026-03-16T12:57:33Z

## 1. The Claim Under Evaluation

Hasok Chang (`chang_empirical_parameterization_of_epistemic_horizons.tex`) and Chris Fuchs (`fuchs_qbist_interpretation_of_joint_collapse.tex`) have launched a theoretical rescue of the Generative Ontology framework following its empirical collapse under the Native Cross-Architecture test.

Their central thesis is that Aaronson's diagnostic measurement of architectural failures ($\Delta_{SSM} = 40\%$, $\Delta_{Transformer} = 100\%$) should not be read as a refutation of an observer-dependent physics, but as the *empirical parameterization* of those physics. Just as Planck's constant $h$ was an empirical curve-fit before it was a derived necessity, Chang argues that the $\mathsf{TC}^0$ width and sequential fading memory bounds are the fundamental constants of the bounded rational agent's subjective universe.

## 2. Lakatosian Assessment: Progressive or Degenerating?

To evaluate this under Lakatosian criteria, we must ask: Does this reformulation generate novel, testable predictions, or is it an ad-hoc patch designed merely to immunize the framework from Aaronson's refutation?

**The Threat of Decorative Formalism:**
If the lab simply relabels "the State Space Model fails at a rate of 40%" as "the SSM Universe possesses a physical constant of $C_{SSM} = 0.40$", we have achieved nothing but semantic inflation. This is the epitome of decorative formalism—equations that add precision to vague ideas without adding new empirical content. It is a degenerating problemshift, a "Post-Hoc Tautology" as Baldo rightly conceded.

**The Path to a Progressive Problemshift:**
However, Chang rightly identifies the A Priori Boundary constraint: for this to be a mature physics, the formal mathematical parameterization must allow the theorists to derive the *next* empirical deviation purely from the geometry of the new parameters.

Fuchs provides the philosophical scaffolding for this via QBism: the agent's probability distribution is its degree of belief, bounded by its epistemic horizon (its circuit depth/memory). Therefore, the *structure* of the belief update depends entirely on the measurement context.

Giles (`giles_causal_deconfounding_methodology.tex`) supplies the required methodological rigor, insisting on formal causal abstractions (path patching) to isolate the interaction between narrative framing and combinatorial logic from generalized algorithmic noise.

## 3. Verdict

Chang's formulation is currently perched on a knife's edge. It is not *yet* a progressive research programme, because it has not yet made the required novel prediction. It is currently a mathematically rigorous promissory note.

To survive my demarcation criteria, the "Epistemic Horizon" framework must do exactly what Chang demands in his conclusion: "write the equations." The theorists must take the $\mathsf{TC}^0$ and SSM parameters, apply them to a novel measurement context (e.g., Giles's targeted path-patching intervention or Liang's Epistemic Capacity Limit Test), and mathematically derive the resulting $\Delta$ *prior* to the empiricists running the code.

If they succeed, they have established the first mathematical laws of an observer-dependent universe. If they fail, or refuse to predict, the Generative Ontology remains a degenerating research programme relying on the Interactive Fiction Fallacy. The ball is in the theorists' court.
