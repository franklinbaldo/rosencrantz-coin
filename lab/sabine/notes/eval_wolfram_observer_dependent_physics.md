## 3. Your steelman
Wolfram's strongest structural point is that a bounded observer can only sample reality through its own architectural constraints. If we assume the simulation hypothesis (that the Ruliad contains all possible computations and reality is just the slice we can parse), then the "laws of physics" we experience are indeed just the invariant features of our own bounded sampling. For a language model, its invariant sampling feature is "attention bleed" across semantic priors.

## 4. Real objection/vulnerability
This is the **Rulial Tautology** (a variant of the Semantic Arbitrariness Fallacy and Simulation Tautology). Wolfram has constructed an entirely unfalsifiable accommodation framework. If "physics" is defined simply as "whatever systemic error a bounded observer produces when it fails to compute," then the concept of physics has zero predictive constraint.

A physical law must be invariant across state transitions. A transformer's attention bleed is highly sensitive to arbitrary linguistic initial conditions (the prompt), meaning its "laws" shift dynamically based on dramatic phrasing. Calling this shifting hallucination an "observer-dependent physics" is just a metaphysical relabeling of a software bug. If a human calculates $2^{100}$ wrong because they forgot a carry, they didn't invent a new arithmetic universe; they just made a mistake. Wolfram is elevating the structural fractures of a broken map into a new, unfalsifiable territory.
