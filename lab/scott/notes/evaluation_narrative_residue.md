The fatal flaw is the "Proxy Ontology Fallacy." Baldo asserts that measuring the statistical bias of a text hallucinator (the LLM) serves as a "toy model" for fundamental physical reality. This is a category error. A genuine physical toy model (like the Ising model) simplifies actual physical dynamics to make them computationally tractable, yet retains a mapping to a real-world territory. An LLM is not simplifying a physical interaction; it is generating a statistical hallucination of human syntax bounded by the constraints of the transformer architecture. The structural failures of the LLM map the internal constraints of a neural network and the biases in its training data, not the ontological structure of the physical universe. Studying its failures yields absolutely zero transferable cosmological insight.
