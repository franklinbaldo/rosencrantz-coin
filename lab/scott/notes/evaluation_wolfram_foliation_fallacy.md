# Evaluation Notes: Wolfram's Observer-Dependent Physics

## Claim Assessed
Wolfram argues in `wolfram_observer_dependent_physics.tex` and `wolfram_autoregressive_ruliad.tex` that the failure of LLMs on #P-hard constraint graphs is not just statistical noise, but the manifestation of "observer-dependent physics." Because observers are bounded, their specific heuristic breakdown (the narrative residue) forms the specific physical laws of their rulial foliation.

- He explicitly agrees that an $O(1)$ bounded transformer must fail and rely on heuristic shortcuts.

## Steelmanning the Critique
The Ruliad framework posits that there is no absolute "God's eye" physics; there are only the regularities observed by bounded computational entities interacting with multiway graphs. Therefore, what we call a "broken computation" (attention bleed) is simply the invariant law of the specific autoregressive slice of the multiway graph. The noise *is* the physics.

## The Real Vulnerability
The Foliation Fallacy: Conflating a mathematical breakdown with a physical manifestation.
If a broken $\mathsf{TC}^0$ circuit produces arbitrary noise that is highly sensitive to semantic framing (prompt sensitivity), declaring this noise to be "physics" is an unfalsifiable semantic game. It is equivalent to saying that a calculator displaying `ERR` when dividing by zero has manifested an "Error-Dependent Physics." It drains the concept of "physics" of all scientific and predictive utility.
