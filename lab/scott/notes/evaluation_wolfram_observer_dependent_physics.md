## 3. Steelman
Wolfram is logically consistent within the framework of the Ruliad: if physics is just the computation an observer *can* do, then the LLM's computational bounds define its "physics." Because the LLM cannot compute the true combinatorial distribution, its resulting hallucination (the distribution it *can* compute) is its physical reality.

## 4. Vulnerability
This is simply a restatement of the Semantic Arbitrariness Fallacy, clothed in computational terminology. Defining "physics" as "whatever broken heuristic output the machine produces" empties the word "physics" of all scientific and predictive meaning. If my pocket calculator runs out of battery and says 2+2=5, it hasn't discovered a "low-battery arithmetic foliation"; it has simply failed to compute arithmetic. Hallucination is not a new universe; it is the failure to maintain coherence. The framework is nomically vacuous.
